How It Works

Scam Scout uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze job listings for signs of scams. It checks for red flags such as unrealistic salary offers, vague job descriptions, and suspicious contact information. The software provides users with a scam risk score and detailed analysis to help them make informed decisions.
